EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Q3 2024 showed strong performance across all revenue streams. Total revenue
reached $2.825M, representing a 12.3% increase over Q2 2024. Operating
expenses were well-managed at $955K, yielding an operating margin of 66.2%.

Key highlights:
- Product revenue grew 8.4% quarter-over-quarter
- Subscription revenue reached $890K, a new quarterly record
- Engineering headcount expanded by 4 FTEs
- Cloud infrastructure costs increased 14% due to scaling initiatives

FORWARD OUTLOOK

Q4 2024 is expected to continue the positive trajectory with projected
revenue of $3.1M–$3.4M driven by year-end subscription renewals and
the launch of the new enterprise product tier. Key risk factors include
potential delays in enterprise deal closures and increased cloud costs
from planned infrastructure expansion.

Capital expenditure for Q4 is budgeted at $180K covering server hardware
upgrades and a new office buildout for the Seattle satellite location.
